Projekte vor der Befreiung

Unsere bisherigen Erfolge im Wiederaufbau Syriens.
Alles noch in Zeiten, wo Hilfe mit Angst und Gefahr verbunden war.

Unsere Erfolge trotz Angst

Durch Unterstützung unserer lokalen Netzwerken und Partner NGOs

15 Notunterkünfte aufgebaut
ShamSy1: Unterkünfte für Vertriebene
Rural Damaskus | 2021–2022

Designed, constructed, and equipped 15 durable shelters for families affected by war in Rural Damascus. Partnered with the Syrian Arab Red Crescent to ensure transparent beneficiary selection.
Medical Operations
ShamSy 3: Lebensrettende Operationen
Damascus | 2015-2023

Funded and coordinated life-changing surgeries and medical interventions for vulnerable patients, including the provision of hearing aids and specialised diagnostic equipment.
School Renovation
ShamSy 4: Schulrenovierung
Der El-Assafir | 2021–2022

Facilitated reopening of a war-damaged school by mobilising volunteers, repairing infrastructure, and restoring safe learning spaces.
Medical Supplies
ShamSy 6: Medizinische Ausrüstung
Douma | 2020-2022

Procured and delivered medicine, critical diagnostic equipment, to enhance emergency and specialised care. Ensured safe delivery and training for hospital staff in Douma.
Food & Workshops
ShamSy 7: Winterhilfe & Bildung
Rural Damascus & Eastern Ghouta | 2017–2024

Provided winter essentials and food to displaced families while enabling youth and women through IT and language workshops.
Pharmaceutical Factory
ShamSy 1: 215 kW Solar System
Rural Damascus | 2023–2024

Contributed technical expertise across all phases to rehabilitate a local pharmaceutical factory, culminating in the installation of a 215 kW solar PV system.
