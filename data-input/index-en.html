Shams Syria e.V.

Syria is a land full of scars — 14 years of war, 55 years of dictatorship, and lack of the most basic necessities of life.

But despite all this darkness, there is hope: People who stayed. People who are returning. People like us, who build bridges between the world and a destroyed Syria.
We support from Germany, which is also our home.
